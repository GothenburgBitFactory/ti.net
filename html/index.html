Support

Q & A

 Your questions have been compiled into a multi-product FAQ. Take a look here - there is a good chance your question is already answered.

Documentation

There is full online documentation to help you learn about all the features and usage.

Issue Tracking

Use our issue tracker on github to see if your problem is reported, fixed, or needs to be reported. Perhaps you have a clarifying example? Insight into a reported problem? Want to request a feature or improvement?

Email

You can ask for help at support@gothenburgbitfactory.org. We request that you first look around at the full online documentation, and perhaps answer your own question. Remember, there is only a small group of volunteers reading these.

Tools

The Timewarrior distribution contains the essential core components. Community-provided extensions will be available here soon.

Join Us

Timewarrior is supported by volunteers. We are an inclusive community and we welcome your feedback. We work together to make the software better. Everything else is out of scope. Please report any abuse.
